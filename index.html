About

Hey! I'm Devin (Nan) Zuo, a software engineer working on autonomous driving, and a nerdy blogger who enjoys reading, learning and problem-solving.

This is a blog where I post reading notes, random thoughts or anything else I wish to share with this world. Keeping a blog has always been on my wish list, inspired by the famous Chinese blogger Weipeng Liu (刘未鹏), who says "writing is the best way of thinking". I really wish I could keep this up as a good learning habit for myself. Meanwhile, it would be my pleasure if this could be useful to any of you.
